# GreenGrocer Data Analysis

## Project Overview
This project was completed for Business Analytics Fundamentals and Applications**. It simulates and analyzes operational data for GreenGrocer, a Florida-based grocery store chain, using business analytics techniques. The project applies descriptive, diagnostic, and predictive analytics to evaluate store performance, customer behavior, and future growth opportunities.

## Objectives
The main goals of this project are to:

- Generate realistic business datasets for multiple store locations
- Analyze sales and profitability performance
- Identify customer shopping patterns and loyalty trends
- Compare operational efficiency across stores
- Build predictive models for sales forecasting
- Develop strategic business recommendations

## Technologies Used
- **Python**
- **Pandas** for data manipulation
- **NumPy** for numerical operations
- **Matplotlib** for visualizations
- **SciPy** for statistical analysis
- **Scikit-learn** for predictive modeling

## Dataset Structure
The project generates and analyzes four primary datasets:

### Store Data
Contains operational details for GreenGrocer locations:

- Store location
- Square footage
- Staff count
- Years in operation
- Weekly marketing spend

### Sales Data
Daily transactional performance including:

- Sales revenue
- Profit margin
- Department/category breakdown
- Seasonal and weekly trends

### Customer Data
Customer demographics and shopping behavior:

- Age and income
- Customer segments
- Monthly spending
- Loyalty tiers
- Preferred store location

### Operational Data
Calculated performance metrics:

- Annual sales
- Annual profit
- Sales per square foot
- Inventory turnover
- Customer satisfaction scores

## Analytics Performed

## Descriptive Analytics
Evaluates current business performance through:

- Total sales and profit calculations
- Average profit margins
- Sales distribution by store
- Department performance comparisons
- Customer segment analysis

## Diagnostic Analytics
Examines relationships between business variables:

- Correlation analysis
- Store efficiency comparisons
- Operational performance rankings
- Seasonal sales trend analysis

## Predictive Analytics
Forecasts future performance using:

- Linear regression sales prediction
- Department growth forecasting
- Model accuracy evaluation (R² score)

## Business Intelligence Insights
The analysis identifies:

- Top-performing store locations
- High-value customer segments
- Most profitable departments
- Seasonal demand fluctuations
- Operational inefficiencies

## Key Findings

### Store Performance
Miami consistently outperforms all other locations in annual sales and profit generation.

### Customer Behavior
Family Shopper and Gourmet Cook segments contribute the highest monthly spending.

### Seasonal Trends
Sales peak during:

- Summer months
- December holiday season
- Weekends

### Department Profitability
Highest-margin departments:

- Prepared Foods
- Produce
- Bakery

## Strategic Recommendations
Based on the analysis, recommended actions include:

- Expand high-performing Miami operations
- Increase weekend staffing
- Launch targeted loyalty campaigns
- Improve inventory strategies for lower-performing stores
- Focus marketing efforts on premium customer segments
